Ice cream cake

Description

Ingredients

  • Cake mix
  • Water
  • Eggs
  • Oil
  • Ice cream

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine chocolate cake mix, water, eggs, and oil in a large bowl. Beat with an electric mixer on medium speed until smooth.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until done.
  4. Set ice cream block on its long side.
  5. Remove cooled cake from the baking dish and place over the ice cream slabs.
  6. Place a board or serving platter over cake. Holding onto the waxed paper and board, flip ice cream cake over
