Lemon bars

Description

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Lemons

Steps

  1. Bake crust in the preheated oven until firm and golden.
  2. make the filling.
  3. Pour filling over the baked crust.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
  5. When cooled, cut bars into uniform squares.
